Platform Posting Schedules

Instagram:

  • Reels: 3-5 times per week (highest reach)
  • Stories: Daily for engagement
  • Feed posts: 4-7 times per week
  • Best times: 11 AM, 2 PM, 7 PM

TikTok:

  • Frequency: 1-3 times daily for algorithm boost
  • Video length: 15-60 seconds performs best
  • Hook viewers in first 3 seconds
  • Jump on trending sounds immediately

Facebook:

  • Post during lunch (12-1 PM) and evening (7-9 PM)
  • Video content gets 135% more reach
  • Focus on community building and groups

LinkedIn:

  • Post 2-3 times per week
  • Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM are peak
  • Document posts (PDF carousels) get high engagement
