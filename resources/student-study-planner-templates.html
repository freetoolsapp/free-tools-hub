Study Techniques You'll Master

Learn proven study methods used by top students worldwide:

  • The Pomodoro Technique: Study for 25 minutes, take 5-minute breaks. Prevents burnout and maintains concentration.
  • Active Recall: Test yourself instead of re-reading. Most effective learning method proven by research. Use flashcards and practice questions.
  • Spaced Repetition: Review material at increasing intervals (Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, Day 14, Day 30). Strengthens long-term memory.
  • The Feynman Technique: Explain concepts in simple terms as if teaching someone. Identifies gaps in understanding.
  • Mind Mapping: Visual representation of information. Great for brainstorming and seeing connections between concepts.
  • Cornell Note-Taking: Divide page into sections for notes, cues, and summary. Organized and effective for review.
  • Time Blocking: Schedule specific study sessions for specific subjects. Reduces decision fatigue.
  • SQ3R Method: Survey, Question, Read, Recite, Review. Systematic approach to reading textbooks.

8-Week Exam Preparation Timeline

Strategic exam preparation schedule:

  • 8 Weeks Before: Gather materials, create study schedule, identify difficult topics
  • 6 Weeks Before: Start active learning, make summary notes, begin practice questions
  • 4 Weeks Before: Intensify practice with past papers, create flashcards, join study groups
  • 2 Weeks Before: Complete full practice exams under timed conditions, review mistakes
  • 1 Week Before: Final review of all materials, light revision, practice exam technique
  • Day Before: Light review of key formulas, get 8 hours sleep, prepare exam materials
