About QR Codes

QR codes (Quick Response codes) are two-dimensional barcodes that can store various types of information including URLs, text, WiFi credentials, contact information, and more. They can be scanned using smartphone cameras to quickly access the encoded data.

Popular Use Cases:

  • Website URLs: Direct customers to your website or landing pages
  • WiFi Sharing: Let guests connect to your WiFi instantly
  • Contact Information: Share vCards with your contact details
  • Marketing: Add to business cards, flyers, and promotional materials
  • Payment: Enable quick mobile payments
  • Product Information: Link to product details and manuals

Tips for Best Results:

  • Use high contrast colors for better scanning
  • Test your QR code before printing
  • Ensure adequate size - minimum 2cm x 2cm for print
  • Add a call-to-action near your QR code
  • Keep URLs short for simpler QR codes
