Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from other Word to PDF converters?

This converter uses docx-preview — the same rendering engine used by Microsoft's own online viewers. It renders the document with pixel-accurate fidelity, then captures each page as a high-resolution image for the PDF. Other converters rebuild the document from scratch and lose formatting.

Will my images and tables be preserved?

Yes. Since we render the document visually first (like a browser would), then capture it as images into the PDF, every element — tables, images, shapes, headers, footers — appears exactly as in Word.

Is my file uploaded to a server?

No. Everything runs in your browser locally. Your file never leaves your device and is never uploaded to any server.

Does it support Urdu, Arabic, and other RTL languages?

Yes. Because we use visual rendering (not text extraction), RTL text, Urdu, Arabic, and any other language renders exactly as it appears in the original Word document.
