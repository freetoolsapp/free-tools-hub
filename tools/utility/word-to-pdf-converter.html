2

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Word to PDF conversion

Is this Word to PDF converter really free?

Yes, our Word to PDF converter is 100% free with no hidden charges, no file size limits, and unlimited conversions. You can convert as many Word documents as you need without any restrictions or premium features locked behind a paywall.

What Word formats are supported?

We support both modern (.docx) and legacy (.doc) Word document formats. Both formats are fully supported with complete formatting preservation, so you can convert any Word document regardless of which version of Microsoft Word it was created in.

Will my document formatting be preserved?

Yes! Our converter preserves all formatting including fonts, colors, images, tables, headers, footers, page breaks, and layout. Your PDF will look identical to your Word document. We use advanced conversion technology to maintain 100% formatting accuracy.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely! All conversions happen directly in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security. No files are uploaded to any server, and we don't store or have access to any of your documents.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation required! This is a 100% web-based tool that works directly in your browser. Just upload your Word file and convert it instantly without downloading or installing any software, plugins, or extensions.

What is the maximum file size I can convert?

There is no strict file size limit. However, very large files (over 50MB) may take longer to process depending on your device's performance. For best results and fastest conversion, we recommend files under 20MB. Most standard documents are well within this range.

Can I convert password-protected Word documents?

Unfortunately, password-protected or encrypted Word documents cannot be converted. You'll need to remove the password protection in Microsoft Word first (File > Info > Protect Document > Encrypt with Password), then convert the unlocked document using our tool.

Does this work on mobile devices?

Yes! Our Word to PDF converter is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. The interface adapts to your screen size for optimal usability on any device.
