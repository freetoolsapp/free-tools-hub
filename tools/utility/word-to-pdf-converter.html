Frequently Asked Questions

Why is this better than other online converters?

Most online converters use JavaScript libraries (like jsPDF or mammoth) that try to re-create the PDF from scratch — they often get fonts, spacing, tables, and page breaks wrong. This tool uses LibreOffice, which is the same rendering engine that major office suites are built on. It reads the Word format natively and produces a perfectly faithful PDF output.

Will tables, images, and page breaks be preserved?

Yes — completely. LibreOffice handles all Word formatting features including tables, embedded images, page breaks, headers, footers, footnotes, columns, text boxes, and all font styles. The output PDF looks identical to the Word document.

Does it support Urdu, Arabic, and other languages?

Yes. LibreOffice has full Unicode support. Urdu, Arabic, Persian, and all other right-to-left languages render exactly as they appear in the original Word document.

Is my file sent to the cloud?

No. Your file is sent only to our own server where LibreOffice processes it. It is deleted immediately after conversion. We do not use any third-party cloud services for conversion.

What is the maximum file size?

Up to 100 MB. This covers virtually all Word documents including those with many images and complex formatting.
