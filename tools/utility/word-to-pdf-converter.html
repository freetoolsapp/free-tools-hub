Frequently Asked Questions

Is this Word to PDF converter really free?

Yes! Our Word to PDF converter is 100% free with no hidden costs. You can convert unlimited files without any signup or subscription required.

What Word formats are supported?

We support both modern DOCX files (Word 2007 and later) and older DOC files (Word 97-2003). You can convert any Word document to PDF.

Are my files secure and private?

Absolutely! All files are processed securely and automatically deleted from our servers immediately after conversion. We don't store or share your documents.

Will the formatting be preserved?

Yes! Our converter maintains the original formatting, fonts, images, tables, and layout. Your PDF will look exactly like your Word document.

What's the maximum file size?

You can convert Word files up to 50MB in size. This is sufficient for most documents including those with images and complex formatting.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation required! Our online converter works entirely in your browser. Just upload your Word document and download the converted PDF.
