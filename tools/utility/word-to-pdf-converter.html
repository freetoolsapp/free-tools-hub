Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How do I convert a Word document to PDF?
Upload your .DOCX or .DOC file using the upload area, then click "Convert to PDF". Your PDF will be ready in seconds. Click the Download button to save it.
Is this Word to PDF converter completely free?
Yes, 100% free. No hidden charges, no subscription, no watermarks, and no file conversion limits. Use it as many times as you need.
Does it support Urdu and Arabic (RTL) text?
Yes! Our converter detects right-to-left languages automatically. Urdu text is rendered using the Noto Nastaliq Urdu font for authentic Nastaliq typography in your PDF.
Will numbered lists (1, 2, 3 and i, ii, iii) appear in the PDF?
Yes! Our engine reads the DOCX numbering.xml to auto-generate decimal (1. 2. 3.), Roman numeral (i. ii. iii.), and alphabetical (a. b. c.) lists exactly as they appear in Word.
Are my documents safe and private?
Absolutely. All conversion happens locally inside your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server. We cannot see, access, or store your documents.
What is the maximum file size supported?
You can upload Word files up to 50MB. This covers the vast majority of documents including those with embedded images and complex formatting.
Does it preserve images in the Word file?
Yes. PNG, JPEG, GIF, and WebP images are embedded directly. EMF/WMF vector images (common in older Word files) are rendered as raster images in the PDF.
