Frequently Asked Questions

How do I crop a PDF?

Upload your PDF, set the crop margins for top, bottom, left, and right sides, then click "Crop PDF". Your cropped PDF will be ready to download.

What are crop margin units?

Margins are measured in points (pts). 72 points equals 1 inch. For example, 36 points = 0.5 inches.

Can I crop specific pages only?

Yes! Enter specific page numbers in the "Pages to Crop" field (e.g., 1,3,5 or 1-5). Leave it empty to crop all pages.

Is my PDF safe?

Yes! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device.
