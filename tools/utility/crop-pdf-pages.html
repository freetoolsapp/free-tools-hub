How do I crop PDF pages online for free?

Can I crop all pages at once?
Yes! The crop selection you make applies to all pages in your PDF document automatically. All pages will be cropped with the same dimensions, saving you time.
Is this PDF cropper tool completely free?
Yes, 100% free forever. No registration, no watermarks, no hidden fees, no file size restrictions (up to 50MB). Use it unlimited times with no restrictions.
Does it work on mobile devices?
Absolutely! Our PDF cropper is fully optimized for touch devices. You can drag and resize the crop box on smartphones and tablets just as easily as on desktop computers.
Are my PDF files safe and private?
Yes, completely safe. All PDF processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server, stored, or shared. They remain 100% private on your device.
