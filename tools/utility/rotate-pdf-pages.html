Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rotate PDF pages?

Upload your PDF, select the pages you want to rotate (or leave blank for all), choose the rotation angle (90°, 180°, 270°), and click rotate. Download your rotated PDF.

Can I rotate only specific pages?

Yes! Enter specific page numbers (e.g., 1,3,5) or use ranges (e.g., 1-5). Leave the field empty to rotate all pages.

What rotation angles are supported?

You can rotate pages by 90° clockwise, 180° (upside down), 270° clockwise (90° counter-clockwise), or 90° counter-clockwise.

Is my PDF safe?

Absolutely! All processing happens in your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy.
