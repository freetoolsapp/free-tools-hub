How to Convert PDF to Word

Follow these simple steps to extract text from your files

1

Upload Your File

Click the "Choose File" button or drag and drop your PDF or image file (JPG, PNG) into the upload area. The tool supports both text-based PDFs and scanned documents/images.

2

Automatic Processing

The tool will automatically detect your file type and process it accordingly. For PDFs, it extracts embedded text. For images and scanned PDFs, it uses OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to recognize text.

3

Review Extracted Text

Once processing is complete, review the extracted text in the editable text area. You can make any necessary corrections or adjustments before downloading.

4

Download Word Document

Click "Download Word Document" to save your extracted text as a .doc file. The formatting and paragraph structure will be preserved. You can also copy the text to your clipboard if needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PDF to Word conversion

Is this PDF to Word converter free?

Yes, our PDF to Word converter is completely free with no hidden charges, unlimited conversions, and no file size restrictions. Convert as many files as you need without any limitations or premium features behind a paywall.

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF files as well as image formats including JPG, JPEG, and PNG. You can convert both text-based PDFs and scanned documents/images to Word format using our OCR technology.

Does this tool include OCR?

Yes! Our converter includes advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology powered by Tesseract.js. This allows you to extract text from scanned PDFs and images automatically with high accuracy.

Will the formatting be preserved?

Yes, we preserve formatting including paragraphs, line breaks, and text structure. The extracted text maintains its original layout as much as possible when converted to Word format. You can also edit the text before downloading.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! All conversions happen directly in your browser using client-side processing. Your files never leave your device and are not uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What is the maximum file size?

We recommend files under 10MB for optimal performance. Larger files may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities. Most standard documents are well within this limit.

Can I edit the extracted text before downloading?

Yes! After extraction, you can review and edit the text directly in the preview window before downloading. This allows you to make any necessary corrections or adjustments to improve accuracy.

Does this work on mobile devices?

Yes! Our PDF to Word converter is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface adapts perfectly to your screen size for optimal usability.
