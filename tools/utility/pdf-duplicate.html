Frequently Asked Questions

How do I duplicate PDF pages?

Upload your PDF, enter the page numbers you want to duplicate, specify how many copies, and click the duplicate button. Download your new PDF with duplicated pages.

Is it safe to upload my PDF?

Yes! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I duplicate multiple pages at once?

Yes! You can enter multiple page numbers separated by commas (e.g., 1,3,5) or use ranges (e.g., 1-5) to duplicate multiple pages at once.

What is the maximum file size?

You can upload PDF files up to 50MB in size. This is sufficient for most documents.
