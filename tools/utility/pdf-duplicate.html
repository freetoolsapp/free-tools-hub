Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about PDF page duplication

How do I duplicate PDF pages online?
Upload your PDF, enter the page numbers you want to duplicate (e.g. 1, 3, 5-7), set the number of copies, and click "Duplicate Pages". Your updated PDF will be ready to download instantly.
Is this PDF duplicator completely free?
Yes, 100% free. No registration required, no watermarks added, and no file conversion limits. Use it as many times as you need without any cost.
Can I duplicate multiple pages at once?
Yes! You can enter multiple page numbers separated by commas (e.g. 1, 3, 5) or use ranges (e.g. 2-5) to duplicate several pages at once. You can even combine both: "1, 3-5, 7".
Where are the duplicated pages inserted?
Each duplicated page is inserted immediately after its original. So if you duplicate page 3, the copy appears right after page 3 in the new PDF, keeping everything in logical order.
Are my PDF files safe and private?
Absolutely. All processing happens locally inside your browser using PDF-lib. Your files are never uploaded to any server â€” we cannot see, access, or store your documents.
What is the maximum file size supported?
You can upload PDF files up to 50MB. This is sufficient for most documents including those with embedded images and complex layouts.
