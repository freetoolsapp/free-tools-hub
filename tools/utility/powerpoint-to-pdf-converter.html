PowerPoint files require special processing. Here are the best methods to convert your presentation to PDF:

Method 1: Using PowerPoint (Recommended)
  1. Open your presentation in PowerPoint
  2. Click File → Export → Create PDF/XPS
  3. Choose your quality settings
  4. Click "Publish" to save as PDF
Method 2: Print to PDF
  1. Open your presentation in PowerPoint
  2. Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac)
  3. Select "Microsoft Print to PDF" or "Save as PDF"
  4. Click "Print" to save your PDF
Method 3: Online Services

Use trusted online converters:

  • Smallpdf.com - Free with limitations
  • ILovePDF.com - Multiple file support
  • Adobe Acrobat Online - Official Adobe tool
  • Zamzar.com - Email-based conversion
Method 4: Google Slides
  1. Upload your PPTX to Google Drive
  2. Open with Google Slides
  3. Click File → Download → PDF Document

Note: Browser-based PowerPoint conversion requires server-side processing due to the complex PPTX file format. The methods above ensure the best quality and maintain all formatting, animations, and transitions.
