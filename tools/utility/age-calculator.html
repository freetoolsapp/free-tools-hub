Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I calculate my exact age?
Simply select your birth day, month, and year from the dropdowns above, then click "Calculate My Age". The calculator will instantly show your exact age in years, months, days, and even hours, minutes, and seconds.
Is this age calculator accurate?
Yes! Our age calculator is 100% accurate. It calculates your chronological age by computing the exact difference between your birth date and the current date, properly accounting for leap years and varying month lengths.
Can I see my age in months and days?
Absolutely! Our calculator shows your complete age breakdown including years, months, and days. It also provides your total age in weeks, days, hours, minutes, and seconds for a comprehensive view.
Does it show my next birthday?
Yes! After calculating your age, the tool automatically displays your next birthday date along with a countdown showing exactly how many days remain until your next birthday celebration.
Is my data saved or shared?
No, we respect your privacy completely. Your birth date is only used for the calculation and is not saved, stored, or shared with anyone. All calculations happen in your browser locally.
Can I use this calculator on mobile?
Yes! Our age calculator is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. The interface adapts to your screen size automatically.
What is chronological age?
Chronological age is your actual age based on your date of birth. It's the amount of time that has passed since you were born, calculated in years, months, and days. Our calculator provides this exact measurement.
Does it work for all countries?
Yes! Our age calculator works universally for everyone regardless of country or location. It uses the standard Gregorian calendar system used worldwide for date calculations.
