About Age Calculator

Our free online age calculator is the most accurate tool to calculate your exact age in years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds. Whether you need to know your chronological age for official documents, birthday planning, or just curiosity, our calculator provides instant and precise results.

How to Use the Age Calculator:

  1. Select your birth day from the dropdown menu
  2. Choose your birth month
  3. Select your birth year
  4. Click "Calculate My Age" button
  5. View your exact age with detailed breakdown

What You'll Get:

  • Exact Age: Your precise age in years, months, and days
  • Total Time Lived: Complete breakdown in months, weeks, days, hours, minutes, and seconds
  • Next Birthday: Find out when your next birthday is and how many days until then
  • Age in Different Units: See your age calculated in various time units

Why Use Our Age Calculator?

For Official Documents: Many official forms require exact age in years, months, and days. Our calculator provides this information accurately.

For Birthday Planning: Know exactly how many days until your next birthday and plan celebrations accordingly.

For Age Verification: Quickly verify if you meet age requirements for various services, events, or applications.

For Fun & Curiosity: Discover interesting facts like how many days, hours, or minutes you've been alive!

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this age calculator?

Our age calculator is 100% accurate. It accounts for leap years and varying month lengths to provide precise calculations.

Does it calculate age in Korean or Chinese system?

This calculator uses the Western (international) age system. For Korean or Chinese age calculations, different methods are used.

Can I calculate someone else's age?

Yes! Simply enter any date of birth to calculate that person's age.

Is my data stored or saved?

No, all calculations are done in your browser. We don't store or save any information you enter.

What if I was born on February 29 (leap day)?

Our calculator handles leap day births correctly and will show your next birthday on either February 28 or March 1 in non-leap years.
