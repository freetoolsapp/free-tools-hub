Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reorder PDF pages?

Upload your PDF, then simply click and drag the page thumbnails to rearrange them in your desired order. Click "Apply New Order" to create the reordered PDF.

Can I see the pages before reordering?

Yes! The tool displays thumbnails of all pages so you can visually see what you're organizing.

Is my PDF secure?

Absolutely! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What if I make a mistake?

You can click the "Reset" button to restore the original page order at any time before applying the changes.
