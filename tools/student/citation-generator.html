Citation Format Guide

APA Format (7th Edition)

American Psychological Association style. Common in psychology, education, and social sciences.

Author, A. A. (Year). Title of work. Publisher.

MLA Format (9th Edition)

Modern Language Association style. Common in humanities and liberal arts.

Author. Title. Publisher, Year.

Chicago Style

Chicago Manual of Style. Common in history, business, and fine arts.

Author. Title. Place: Publisher, Year.

Harvard Style

Harvard referencing system. Common in UK universities and sciences.

Author, A. (Year) Title. Place: Publisher.
