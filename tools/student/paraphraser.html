Frequently Asked Questions

What is paraphrasing?

Paraphrasing is the process of rewriting text in different words while maintaining the original meaning. It's useful for avoiding plagiarism, creating unique content, and improving text clarity.

Is the paraphraser free to use?

Yes! Our paraphraser is completely free with no signup or subscription required. You can paraphrase unlimited text without any restrictions.

Will the paraphrased text be plagiarism-free?

Our AI creates unique content by rewriting text with different words and sentence structures, making it plagiarism-free. However, we recommend running it through a plagiarism checker for important academic or professional documents.

What's the difference between paraphrase modes?

Standard mode provides balanced rewriting, Fluent makes text more natural and readable, Creative makes more significant changes, Formal uses professional language, and Simple uses easier-to-understand words.

Is there a word limit?

No, you can paraphrase text of any length. For very long documents (10,000+ words), we recommend paraphrasing in sections for best results.

Can I use paraphrased content for my assignments?

While our tool helps create unique content, you should always cite sources appropriately and follow your institution's academic integrity guidelines. Paraphrasing should be used ethically and responsibly.
