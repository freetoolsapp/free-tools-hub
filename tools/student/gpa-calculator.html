How to Use GPA Calculator

  1. Select your GPA scale (4.0 or 5.0)
  2. Enter course name (optional)
  3. Select the grade you received
  4. Enter credit hours for each course
  5. Click "Add Another Course" to add more courses
  6. Click "Calculate GPA" to see your result

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GPA?

GPA stands for Grade Point Average. It's a numerical representation of your academic performance.

Is this calculator accurate?

Yes! Our calculator uses standard GPA calculation methods used by most institutions.

Can I calculate cumulative GPA?

Yes, simply enter all your courses from all semesters to calculate your cumulative GPA.
