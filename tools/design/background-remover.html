Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remove background from an image for free?
Simply upload your image using the upload area above, wait 2-5 seconds for automatic AI processing, then download your image with a transparent background as PNG. It's completely free with no signup required.
What is the maximum file size I can upload?
You can upload images up to 50MB in size. This is large enough for most high-resolution photos and professional images while ensuring fast processing speeds.
Which image formats are supported?
We support PNG, JPG, JPEG, and WEBP formats as input. The output is always a high-quality PNG file with transparent background for maximum compatibility and quality.
Are my images safe and private?
Yes, absolutely. All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server, stored, or shared. They remain completely private on your device.
